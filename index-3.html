Cheshire Cat

Cheshire Cat was blink-182's debut studio album. It was recorded at Westbeach Recorders. It was released by the record label Cargo Music. This album was named after the famous character from Alice in Wonderland, which was DeLonge's favorite character. By 2001, the album had sold over 250,000 copies. The release of the album was considered by many to be a highly iconic release. However, at the time of release, blink-182 was a relatively unknown band, and not many reviews were written about it. After its meteoric rise, many top rock magazines were regretting their decision to write off blink-182.
